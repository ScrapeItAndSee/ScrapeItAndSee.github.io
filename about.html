About Me

A summary of my journey up to now

Emmanuel Rodriguez

Math Teacher Turned Data Scientist

Growing up, I’ve always been into growing my mind by solving puzzles and challenges, keeping my heart healthy by staying active in sports, and satisfying my soul by writing, recording, and performing music. As I got older, these things haven’t really changed. I still take some time to stay active and learn about nutrition, I still write, record and perfrom music occasionally, and I am currently looking for the next challenge/ mental growth, and hoping that I can find it in the realm of Data Science.

I was born and raised, and currently live, in the New York City area on the other side of the river in a town called North Bergen. After graduating from William Paterson University with B.S. in Computer Science and minor in math, I explored my passion for sharing knowledge by becoming a high school math teacher. I’ve been helping students face quantitative challenges for about three and a half years, and now it’s time for me to seek some challenges of my own in the field of Data Science.

When I'm not working, I do my best to spend time in one of my many hobies. I like to write, record, and perform with one of two bands I'm apart of. In one band, I sing half the songs and play acoustic guitar or keys along side 4 other members, one of which sings the other half of our songs. In the other band, I play drums and do some background singing for a friend. When I am looking for a fun challenge, I dive with a couple of friends into one of the 50+ board games that I have in my collection. Lastly, to stay fit, I like to perfect my skills in Jiu Jitsu or just go for a pieceful jog around the park.

Check out my most recent project on GitHub, or connect with me on LinkedIn.

Thanks for reading and welcome to my page!
